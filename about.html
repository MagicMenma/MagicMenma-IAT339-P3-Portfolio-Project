3D Digital Designer

As a person with an open-minded view of beauty, I am passionate about beautifully designed clothing for both men and women. This obsession with aesthetics has driven me to maintain high standards throughout my studies as a 3D digital designer. With diverse experience in digital model design, including scene, industrial design, and character modeling, I strive to create visually compelling and emotionally engaging designs that connect with people. This drive is reflected in my visual and textual ethos, which showcases my ability to think through a problem and iterate on a project over time to create innovative and impactful designs.
