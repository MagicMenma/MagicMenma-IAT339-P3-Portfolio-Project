About Us

Chiliken

Chiliken is a fried chicken fast food restaurant. It offer the special spicy taste chicken product. There are multiple level of spicy that consumers can try. The Devil's Pepper grade will be available for a limited time each year for consumer challenges. It also offers a non-spicy menu including French fries, salads, desserts and a variety of soft drinks.

Potential Customers

  1. The person between 8 – 40 years old who love fried chicken.
  2. The person who love spicy food or chili pepper.
  3. The person don’t have time to prepare food.
  4. The person who is not so concerned about the health(Calories) of food.

Consumer motivation

  1. A unique fresh spicy flavor that you can't find in the market.
  2. Attractive promotional deals and discounts.
